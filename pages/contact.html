Mythos Cradle

As a sanctuary and adoption agency, we specialize in connecting families with magical creatures, carefully matching each little legend to the caretakers who will best support their growth and well-being. From playful blue dragons to gentle forest spirits, every adoption is designed to foster wonder, curiosity, and companionship. With a commitment to ethical care, safety, and magical enrichment, we strive to create joyful experiences for both creatures and their new families.
